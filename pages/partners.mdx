## Overview

Anyone can make money with ExaLotto, and not just by winning!

ExaLotto is managed as a DAO (_"Decentralized Autonomous Organization"_) and anyone can join. Voting
power is measured in `EXL`, an [ERC20][erc20] token that you can [trade on Uniswap][exl-uniswap].
